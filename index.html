Our Story

How we met

We met in Rock Island, Illinois on May 18, 2013 at the house of Alderman Joshua Shipp. If not love at first sight, it was love after an hour of hanging out. So we made our first date for 5:30 in the morning the next Sunday to have enough time for our 6 hour road trip to Devils Lake. That way if we didn't like each other, it wouldn't take us too long to find out.

It's hard being in love and living in seperate states, but not even the vast plains of Northern Illinois could keep us apart. We were planning on moving to Colorado, but Gus had other plans. So, we moved to the next best place, Chicago, IL. It wasn't easy at first... or second, but things are pretty good now, and they keep getting better.

Since then, we have settled into our neighborhood nicley. Melanie teaches yoga a block away, Alex has met many friends through coding, and everyone in the neighborhood seems to know Gus.
